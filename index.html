Who We Are

Connectivity & Hosting Excellence

Located in Blumenau, Santa Catarina, WERT FINANCE LTDA is a specialized provider of telecommunications and computing infrastructure. Led by Guilherme De Souza Meirelles, we focus on delivering robust digital foundations for businesses.

Despite our name "Wert Finance" (implying Value Finance), our core expertise lies in "Wired and Wireless Telecommunications" and "Internet Host Services." We provide the critical data processing and hosting capabilities that financial, media, and tech companies rely on.

We combine German precision—a hallmark of the Blumenau region—with cutting-edge technology to offer secure, high-speed data transmission and reliable server hosting environments.
